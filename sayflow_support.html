SayFlow — Support

If you need help with SayFlow, please contact our support team. Provide a short description of the issue and, if possible, include device model and iOS version.

Quick troubleshooting

  • Restart the app and try again.
  • Make sure you have the latest app version installed.
  • If the problem relates to purchases, sign in to the App Store with your Apple ID and use "Restore Purchases" in the app.
